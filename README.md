# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

An interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** developed in **Tableau** to analyze employee attrition and workforce data. The dashboard provides key HR metrics and visual insights that help understand employee turnover, workforce demographics, and job satisfaction.

---

# 📌 Problem Statement

Employee attrition is a significant challenge for organizations as it leads to increased hiring costs, loss of experienced employees, and reduced productivity. HR departments require an efficient way to monitor employee turnover and identify trends across different workforce dimensions.

This project aims to develop an interactive Tableau dashboard that consolidates HR data into meaningful visualizations. The dashboard enables HR professionals to track key workforce KPIs, analyze attrition by department, gender, education field, and age group, and evaluate job satisfaction across different job roles to support informed decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to create an interactive Tableau dashboard that helps HR teams:

- Monitor overall workforce statistics.
- Measure employee attrition and attrition rate.
- Analyze attrition across different departments.
- Understand employee distribution by age group.
- Evaluate job satisfaction by job role.
- Analyze attrition based on gender and education field.

# 📌 Key Insights

- Total employees in the organization: **1,470**
- Total employee attrition: **237**
- Overall attrition rate: **16.12%**
- Active employees: **1,233**
- Average employee age: **37 years**
- Sales department has the highest attrition compared to HR and R&D.
- Employees aged around **30–36 years** represent the largest workforce segment.
- Life Sciences and Medical education fields contribute the highest attrition.
- The dashboard highlights job satisfaction ratings across different job roles.
- Attrition patterns can be analyzed by gender across multiple age groups.
